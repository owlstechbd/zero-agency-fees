Our Process

We keep things simple, so all you need to do is wait for your rent payment each month. Our three-step process makes sure of that!


Step - 1

Consultation

Contact us to arrange a consultation with an account manager in your property. This is your chance to find out more about our approach to property letting and discuss issues such as rent.


Step - 2

Trial

If you’re happy with the rent being offered and our approach to zero-fee, zero-hassle property lettings, we will set up a short, no-obligation trial — giving you the chance to see how things work.


Step - 3

Sign Up

Once we’ve both had the chance to understand one another’s expectations — and the particulars of the property — we will formally offer you a guaranteed, zero-fee monthly rent.
